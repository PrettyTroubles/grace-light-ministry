About Grace & Light

We are a prophetic ministry rooted in love, worship, and bold identity. Our calling is to awaken hearts, restore hope, and empower every believer to walk in their divine purpose.

“The righteous are bold as a lion.” — Proverbs 28:1
  • 🕊️ Spirit-led worship that invites Heaven to earth
  • 🤝 Outreach that transforms lives through compassion
  • 🦁 Prophetic identity that awakens your roar
  • 🌟 Fellowship that builds lasting spiritual family

Come as you are—and leave ignited. You were never meant to blend in. You were born to shine.
